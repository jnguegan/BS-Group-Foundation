Project Location & Institutional Status

This project is implemented in partnership with an established Primary and Secondary Community School located in the Adamaoua Region, Cameroon.

The school currently operates as a fully authorized Primary School. The Secondary School is under development and is not yet operational due to funding, ongoing administrative paperwork and authorization requirements.
