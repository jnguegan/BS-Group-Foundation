Flagship Project: School Infrastructure Completion

BS Group Foundation is supporting the completion of a Primary and Secondary Community School campus in the Adamaoua Region of Cameroon, integrating education, vocational skills and sport development.

Sponsor a School Donate Contact

Primary School is operational. Secondary School infrastructure is in place and currently in the final administrative and authorization phase before opening.

Project Location & Institutional Status

This project is implemented in partnership with an established Primary and Secondary Community School located in the Adamaoua Region, Cameroon.

The school currently operates as a fully authorized Primary School. The Secondary School is under development and is not yet operational due to ongoing administrative paperwork and authorization requirements.
