Transparency

The BS Group Foundation is committed to transparency, accountability, and responsible management of its activities and initiatives.

Use of Funds

Funds received by the Foundation may be used to support educational programs, youth development initiatives, community projects, and operational activities necessary to deliver these programs.

International Activities

The BS Group Foundation operates internationally and may support initiatives and programs in multiple countries aligned with its mission.

Donations

All donations to the Foundation are voluntary and support the charitable mission of the organization.

Donation tax deductibility may depend on the donor’s jurisdiction and applicable regulations.
