About the Gala

Gala de L’Impact 2026 – Unir pour l’Avenir is the inaugural charity gala of BS Group Foundation in Paris, bringing together leaders, professional football players, diaspora communities, and corporate partners to support sustainable impact programs across Africa.

Our Mission

The gala supports BS Group Foundation’s core pillars: education, health, sport, women empowerment, skills development, and entrepreneurship.

Who Will Attend

High-level professionals, corporate sponsors, athletes, investors, institutional partners, and influential members of the diaspora.

Why Attend

Join an exceptional evening of networking, visibility, philanthropy, and premium engagement in one of Europe’s leading capitals.

What Your Presence Supports

  • Education access and school support initiatives
  • Youth development through sport
  • Health and community support initiatives
  • Women empowerment and leadership programs
  • Skills development, training, and entrepreneurship
